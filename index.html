My Story

In my many jobs in the tourism industry, from giving guided tours of historic houses to assisting Disney characters in their meet and greets, I have seen how the smallest details can help make an experience more immersive and memorable.

By applying these skills to web design and development, I am able to make informed choices that will add to the user experience, and create websites with that same sense of storytelling.
